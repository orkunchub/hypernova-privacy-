Hypernova is developed by Veritas Plus. This application does not collect, process, or share any personal data.

The app does not request any personal information from users. There is no account creation, registration, or login system. Your high score is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.

The app may display advertisements via Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers for ad personalization. This data processing is solely under Google's responsibility. You can review Google's privacy policy at: policies.google.com/privacy
